Bachelor In Computer Application 
            
            The BCA program under the faculties of humanities and social sciences of Tribhuvan University aims to produce skilled computer application users and developers. It spans four years, is divided
            into eight semesters, and is offered in >130 colleges with 4500 seats for enrollment. The medium of instruction and examination of BCA is English.      
            Tribhuvan University has launched Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) program from the academic year 2074/75.  In the first phase, this program was launched in six (6) constituent campuses of Tribhuvan University 
            and are allocated 35 seats each. The program runs on semester-system under the faculties of Humanities and Social Sciences. 
            
            The objective of the BCA program of Tribhuvan University is to produce high quality computer application
            users and developers. The program of study for Bachelor of Arts in Computer Application (BCA) is over a 
            period of eight semesters (four academic years). The academic year begins in September and February of each
            year. The medium of instruction and examination in the Bachelor of Arts in Computer Application (BCA) program
            is English. 

           
 BCA Scope and Career Prospects


           In recent years, BCA in Nepal have grown to become one of the most popular computer science undergraduate degree and provides students with wide range of career prospects and scope for growth within their career later on. Students after graduating can have career in fields of computer science such as programming, software development, computer networks, artificial intelligence, quality assurance, DevOps, databases, application & web development and much more.

After BCA: Your Next Steps


            Embark on a dynamic journey after completing your Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) with these exciting options:
            
1.Master Your Craft:

            Elevate your expertise with a Master's in Computer Application (MCA) for in-depth knowledge and enhanced career opportunities.
            
            
2.Specialize Your Skills:

            Tailor your career path by opting for specialized courses or certifications in areas like web development, data science, AI, and cybersecurity.
            
            
3.Dive into the Industry:

            Gain hands-on experience through internships or entry-level positions, translating your academic knowledge into real-world skills.
            
            
4.Tech Entrepreneurship:

            Bring your ideas to life by venturing into entrepreneurship, whether it's a software development company, tech startup, or another IT-driven business.
            
            
5.Government Roles:

            Explore fulfilling opportunities in the public sector, where IT professionals are often sought after for roles in public service, defense, and government agencies.
            
            
6.Freelance Freedom:

            Embrace flexibility with freelance opportunities, taking on projects aligned with your interests and schedule.
            
            
7.Global Adventures:

            Explore international job opportunities, leveraging your skills on a global scale for a unique and enriching experience.
            Continuous Growth:
            
            Stay ahead by networking, participating in professional associations, and continuous skill development to thrive in the ever-evolving world of computer applications.
            Your BCA is just the beginning; discover the multitude of paths that await you and shape a rewarding career in the dynamic realm of technology.
