Texas International College

Texas International College, part of Texas International Education Network since 2009, is a high-standard academic institution managed by capable and energetic professionals. With a motto of "Education for a complete human being," it offers quality education in management, science, and humanities. Located in Mitrapark, Chabahil, Kathmandu Valley, the college aims to produce globally competitive professionals aligned with societal needs, fostering excellence in research, interactivity, and diversified knowledge.
