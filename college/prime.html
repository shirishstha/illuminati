Prime College

Prime College is an educational institution striving to deliver practical-based education, empowering young minds and cultivating a culture of excellence among skilled individuals. We offer diverse academic programs at both undergraduate and graduate levels. Our dedicated faculty members bring their extensive expertise to the classroom, fostering a dynamic learning environment. They aspire to continue as one of the leading educational institutions where students not only develop intellectually but also gain knowledge and skills to become impactful global citizens.


Facilities:


Library: Prime College has a well-equipped library where students can come up with new ideas. We also have over 300 internship reports, over 450 fieldwork reports, and over 150 summer projects. The library at Prime College aids students in their academic areas as well as obtain extra enlightenment in a tranquil setting by providing them with a wide and comfortable place.


Computer Lab: Prime College's computer lab serves as a study station and a learning destination to assist students in achieving their goals of research, creativity, and execution. The computer lab serves as a great place for students to work on their projects, research, and presentations because it provides them with up-to-date and high-quality computing tools, gadgets, and software, as well as high-speed internet technology.


Auditorium Hall Prime College's auditorium is a central hub for various activities, including debates, lectures, presentations, workshops, and events. Fully equipped with a sound system, display setup, and comfortable seating, it serves as a vital space for student engagement.


Cafeteria Prime College's cafeteria provides a diverse range of affordable meals and beverages. Operating from early morning to late afternoon, it serves hygienic meals and serves as a meeting place for students. With options for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian choices, students can recharge with a variety of refreshments.


Scholarships: Prime College awards scholarships based on academic, athletic, and extracurricular achievements. MBS students with prior professional experience are eligible for work experience-based scholarships. Merit-based scholarships aim to encourage better academic performance. Additionally, the college provides Bachelor's degree scholarships for students from low-income backgrounds. Admissions for Bachelors and Masters programs open after relevant exam results are published, with interested students welcome to visit for program details on weekdays from 7:00 am to 5:00 pm.
