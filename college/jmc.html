Janamaitri Multiple Campus

Janamaitri Multiple Campus is a QAA Certified Institute, Accredited by UGC, Nepal. The college boasts highly qualified and experienced faculty, utilizing innovative teaching methods with multimedia. It implements regular internal evaluations, offers personalized education, and provides remedial classes for struggling students. The well-equipped library and laboratories, spacious classrooms, and ideal environment enhance the learning experience. Affordable fees and scholarships support meritorious students, complemented by counseling services and intellectual workshops. Periodical sports and extracurricular activities contribute to a well-rounded education. With a broad subject choice, career-oriented courses, and hands-on approaches like project works and field trips, the college ensures a comprehensive educational journey.


Scholarship information: The college recognizes academic achievements by awarding gold medals and cash prizes to board exam toppers at each level. Faculty ranking 1st to 3rd in final exams receive partial scholarships. Students must attend and pass all internal examinations to be eligible for scholarships and college facilities. Maintaining a specified percentage in the 1st year's annual exam is necessary for the second part of the scholarship.
