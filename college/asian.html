Asian Scool of management and Technology

Asian College of Management, also known as ACMT, was established to meet the growing demand for IT professionals in Nepal. Founded by experienced individuals, ACMT emphasizes technical and skill-oriented learning over traditional lectures. The college distinguishes itself by providing world-class education, enabling students to compete internationally. ACMT offers a range of undergraduate and graduate courses delivered by highly qualified faculty members under the affiliation with Tribhuvan University. The institution values the enthusiasm of Nepali students for IT and ensures that courses are designed to be affordable without compromising the quality of education.

Clubs:
The college offers various student clubs, including the Robotics Club and Social Service Club. Students can actively participate in these clubs. Additionally, the college hosts an annual intercollege mega event called "Asian Hackathon."

Cafeteria:
The college has a clean and hygienic canteen where students can access healthy and nutritious food at an affordable price.

Sports:
ASMT promotes student involvement in sports activities and hosts an annual sports meet. The college provides facilities like a basketball court, table tennis, and badminton court on the premises for students to engage in sporting events.

semianr and workshops:
In addition to regular lectures, ASMT organizes seminars and workshops to enhance students' professional skills. Students are encouraged to participate in events relevant to their field of study. The college also frequently invites renowned professionals and experts for guest lectures, providing valuable insights beyond the regular curriculum.

Library and computer lab:
The college features two well-equipped IT labs, providing practical training to keep students updated on the latest technology trends. Additionally, ASMT has specialized labs, including a Networking Lab, Robotics Lab, and Digital Lab, for hands-on experience in relevant fields. The college has a well-stocked library where students can borrow their text books and related reading materials. There is a reading zone with a number of periodicals and newspapers.

Schoolorships:
ASMT offers a 100% scholarship for the first semester's tuition fee to students securing 80% and above in their +2 or equivalent exams. Internal evaluation toppers receive a 75% scholarship for the highest scorer and a 50% scholarship for the second-highest, fostering academic excellence and healthy competition.
