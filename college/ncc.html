Nepal Commerce Campus(NCC)

Nepal Commerce Campus (NCC), a well-known management college of Tribhuvan University, began in 1964 with a mission to provide quality education. Situated in New-Baneshwor, Kathmandu, it has played a vital role in the country's industrial and commercial growth by producing skilled managers. The campus, in the heart of Kathmandu, boasts an experienced team of professors. Over the years, it has graduated numerous professionals now serving in civil services, private sectors, and esteemed national and international institutions.

Library:
The campus offers a rich physical library open from 6:30 AM to 5:00 PM on regular days, housing 65,000 books, 4 journals, reports, audiovisual materials, maps, and photographs. Complementing this, a well-connected digital library provides access to various online resources, all supported by broadband Internet and Wi-Fi.

Cafeteria:
The cafeteria serves affordable and healthy food.
