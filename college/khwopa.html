Khwopa College

Khwopa College, named after the historic town Bhaktapur's ancient name, is the sole community-based service-oriented college governed by the local government, Bhaktapur Municipality, since its establishment in 1999 AD. The college is dedicated to providing quality and practical education at an affordable and lower fee rate, countering the rising costs of education in the nation. In pursuit of its mission to make education a basic necessity, the institution, situated in the cultural city of Bhaktapur, has welcomed students from all 65 districts, recognizing the ongoing need to further expand its reach.
