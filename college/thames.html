Thames International College

Thames International College, located in Purano Baneshwor, Kathmandu, is recognized for its high-quality academic programs, offering a range of bachelor programs. Situated in a bustling area with a diverse society, it attracts students from all corners of Nepal. The college is organized into two schools based on the field of study: the School of Business and Technology, and Social Sciences. Known for its education excellence, Thames International College has built a strong reputation, thanks to the dedication of experienced academicians and an excellent learning environment. The college focuses on preparing students to tackle real-world challenges and encourages independent decision-making. Special classrooms are designed to promote interactive learning for individuals.


INDUCTION SEMINAR :
Students at this college can join an Induction Seminar for a smooth transition. It covers administration, faculty, policies, resources, and student engagement opportunities.


FIRST YEAR SEMINAR :
Active participation in First-Year Seminar Courses prepares students for lifelong learning. Courses include Reading and Writing for College, Academic Writing, Critical Thinking, Interactive Approach to Academic Reading, Case-Based Approach to Learning, and Foundation of Mathematics.


ACADEMIC ADVISING:
Students are assigned Academic Advisors in their first year for support and access to essential information and services. Advisors meet regularly, assisting in creating personalized success plans to achieve academic goals.


STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS:
Thames International College's Student Council offers an integrated experience, providing diverse clubs and organizations to enrich campus life. Students can enhance their academic program through representation in the Student Council.Currently, there are four student clubs: 

Student Council
             -Rotaract Club
             -Management Club
             -Information Technology Club


            
            
CULTURAL EXCHANGE:

            Thames students can engage in global travel through the cultural exchange program. Annual events include International Students' Meet in Rourkela, Orissa, and Jeunes Du Monde in Toulouse, France. Chinese language class offers opportunities for students to travel to China.


            
            
Scholarships:

            College scholarship programs provide vital financial support for students. Various scholarships are offered, requiring applications with academic transcripts, test scores, essays, and recommendation letters. Some may involve additional materials or interviews. Researching and applying for eligible scholarships is crucial to alleviate financial stress and allow students to concentrate on studies and activities.
