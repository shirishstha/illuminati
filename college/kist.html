KIST College of Management 
          KIST College of MAnagement
          Kathmandu Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) College, founded in 1995 and situated in Kamalpokhari, Kathmandu, is a highly regarded academic institution for +2, Bachelor's, and Master's level students. With a commitment to multidimensional education, KIST aims to produce competent alumni in both science and management fields. Known for its dedicated faculty and unique teaching methods, KIST has earned a reputation for delivering high-quality education at par with the best institutions, making it a preferred choice for students and families.
          
          ICT Center: The college has a digitally networked working environment and continuous flow of internet internet alongside well-equipped computer labs.
          
          Library:
          The library at KIST consists of standardized textbooks and general reading books. There is a wide collection of reference books and e-library/ e-gadgetry.
          
          Canteen: The Canteen at KIST provides healthy and affordable food and beverages.
          
          Extracurricular Activities: It is a happening place to be. Life at KIST is a combination of study, club activities, and events. They get the opportunity to be part of multiple activities and events throughout the year.
          
          Games and Sports: table tennis, cricket, badminton, futsal and basketball regale students.
          
          Laboratories: The College has state-of-the-art computer & IT laboratories fully equipped according to the norms & standards of the Universities to which its courses bear affiliation. It has basic computer labs along with a digital logic lab and network lab.
          
          Scholarships:
          KIST awards Scholarships to students who get high marks in +2/ Equivalent-level exams & Entrance Exams. These Scholarships are allotted in order of mark-based merit per seat availability by given deadlines.
          
          Location: Kamalpokhari,Kathmandu
